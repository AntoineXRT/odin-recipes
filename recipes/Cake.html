Chocolate Cake

This is a chocolate cake made from the finest ingredients that mother nature provides

We hope you enjoy it

Ingredients

  • Cocoa
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Creme
  • Water
  • Vanila

Steps ;

  1. Mix flour with water
  2. Add in Baking Soda
  3. add 3ml of Milk
  4. Add 15g of Cocoa Powder
  5. Add Vanila for flavour
  6. Bake for 30 minutes
